### [1] [Radiative decays of the 1$P$, 1$D$, 2$S$, and 2$P$ $Λ_c$ and 1$D$, 2$S$, and 2$P$ $Ξ_c$ charmed baryons](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13903)
*R. Gamboa-Goni,Ailier Rivero-Acosta,H. García-Tecocoatzi,A. Gutierrez-Rodriguez,A. Ramirez-Morales,E. Santopinto,Carlos Alberto Vaquera-Araujo*

Main category: hep-ph

TL;DR: This paper computes electromagnetic radiative decay widths and branching ratios for excited singly charmed baryons ($Λ_c$ and $Ξ_c$) in the flavor anti-triplet using the constituent quark model, with a focus on distinguishing overlapping resonances such as $Ξ_c(3055)$ and $Ξ_c(3080)$ through their electromagnetic signatures. It presents the first calculations for $D_ρ$-wave states, $ρ$-$λ$ mixed configurations, and radially excited $ρ$-mode states.
\n\n

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Electromagnetic decay widths are crucial for identifying hadronic resonances when multiple states have nearly degenerate masses and total widths, making strong decays insufficient for unambiguous assignments. The recent LHCb observations of $Ξ_c(3055)$ and $Ξ_c(3080)$ require theoretical input to determine their quantum numbers and internal structure, particularly whether $Ξ_c(3080)$ is a 1$D$ ($J^P=5/2^{+}$) or 2$S$ ($J^P=1/2^{+}$) state. A systematic calculation within the constituent quark model fills this gap.
\n\n

Method: The authors employ the non-relativistic constituent quark model to compute electromagnetic transition amplitudes between baryon states. They include wavefunctions for ground states (1$S$), $P$-wave excitations (1$P$), radially excited states (2$S$, 2$P$), and $D$-wave states (1$D$), incorporating both $ρ$- and $λ$-type orbital excitations and their mixing. Transition matrix elements are evaluated using standard electromagnetic operators (electric dipole $E1$, magnetic quadrupole $M2$, etc.), and decay widths are derived from these amplitudes. Both experimental uncertainties (e.g., mass inputs) and model-dependent uncertainties (e.g., quark model parameters) are propagated.
\n\n
作者采用非相对论性组分夸克模型计算重子态之间的电磁跃迁振幅。他们构建了基态（1$S$）、$P$ 波激发态（1$P$）、径向激发态（2$S$、2$P$）以及 $D$ 波态（1$D$）的波函数，并包含 $ρ$ 型和 $λ$ 型轨道激发及其混合。利用标准电磁算符（电偶极 $E1$、磁四极 $M2$ 等）计算跃迁矩阵元，并由此导出衰变宽度。分析中同时考虑了实验不确定性（如质量输入）和模型依赖不确定性（如夸克模型参数）的传播。

Result: The paper provides predictions for radiative decay branching ratios of various $Λ_c$ and $Ξ_c$ excited states. Specifically, it offers testable branching ratios that support the assignment of $Ξ_c(3055)$ as a $J^P=3/2^{-}$ 1$P$ state. For $Ξ_c(3080)$, the results favor its interpretation as the 2$S$ $J^P=1/2^{+}$ state over the 1$D$ $J^P=5/2^{+}$ assignment, based on the relative magnitudes of predicted electromagnetic decay rates. First-ever results are presented for transitions involving $D_ρ$-wave, $ρ$-$λ$ mixed, and radially excited $ρ$-mode configurations.
\n\n

Conclusion: Electromagnetic decays serve as a powerful tool for spectroscopy of singly charmed baryons, especially in cases of overlapping resonances. The constituent quark model successfully describes the pattern of radiative transitions, and the new predictions—particularly for higher orbital and radial excitations—provide crucial benchmarks for future experiments at LHCb, Belle II, and BESIII to clarify the nature of observed $Ξ_c$ states.
\n\n
电磁衰变是单粲重子谱学研究的有力工具，尤其适用于共振态重叠的情形。组分夸克模型成功描述了辐射跃迁的模式，而针对高轨道角动量和径向激发态的新预测为 LHCb、Belle II 和 BESIII 等未来实验提供了关键基准，有助于澄清已观测到的 $Ξ_c$ 态的本质。

Abstract: We analyze the radiative decays of the the 1$P$, 1$D$, 2$S$, and 2$P$ $Λ_c$ and 1$D$, 2$S$, and 2$P$ $Ξ_c$ charmed baryons, which belong to the flavor anti-triplet ($\bf {\bar 3}_{\rm F}$), using the constituent quark model. We compute electromagnetic transitions from ground and $P$-wave states to ground states, as well as from second-shell states to both ground and $P$-wave final states. Electromagnetic decay widths are especially valuable for identifying resonances when multiple states share the same mass and total decay width. We give branching ratios which can confirm the assignment of the $Ξ_c(3055)$ reported by LHCb. We also give branching ratios that can support the assignment of the $Ξ_c(3080)$, and discuss the possibilities for the $Ξ_c(3080)$ to be the 1$D$ state with $J^P=5/2^{+}$ or the 2$S$ with $J^P=1/2^{+}$. For the first time, this work provides calculations of electromagnetic decays for $D_ρ$-wave states, $ρ-λ$ mixed configurations, and $ρ$-mode radially excited states in singly charmed baryons of the flavor anti-triplet. Both experimental and model-dependent uncertainties are taken into account throughout our analysis.

### [2] [Spherical collapse and cluster number counts in DHOST theories that pass the constraints from gravitational waves](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13937)
*Sakdithut Jitpienka,Khamphee Karwan,David F. Mota*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: This paper studies the spherical collapse model and galaxy cluster abundance in degenerate higher-order scalar–tensor (DHOST) theories that evade gravitational-wave decay constraints. It finds that, despite satisfying local and gravitational-wave bounds, these theories suppress small-scale linear growth and enhance the critical density contrast at collapse, leading to fewer high-redshift clusters than in $\\Lambda$CDM when calibrated to eROSITA data.
\n\n
本文研究了一类规避引力波衰变限制的退化高阶标量-张量（DHOST）理论中的球对称塌缩模型和星系团丰度。研究发现，尽管满足局域及引力波观测约束，这些理论会抑制小尺度线性增长并增强塌缩时的临界密度对比度，导致在与eROSITA数据校准后，高红移星系团数量少于$\\Lambda$CDM模型。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: The motivation is to explore observational signatures of viable DHOST theories—specifically those consistent with gravitational-wave speed constraints and local tests of gravity—through their impact on nonlinear structure formation, particularly the abundance of galaxy clusters, which serves as a sensitive probe of modified gravity.
\n\n
动机在于探索符合引力波传播速度限制和局域引力检验的一类可行DHOST理论在非线性结构形成中的观测信号，特别是星系团丰度，因其对修改引力极为敏感。

Method: The authors employ the spherical collapse model within specific DHOST theories that avoid scalar decay of gravitational waves. They compute the linear growth factor and extrapolated linear density contrast at collapse $\\delta_c(z)$, then use an analytic mass function (likely Sheth–Tormen or Press–Schechter) to predict cluster number counts. The minimum halo mass in each redshift bin is fixed by matching $\\Lambda$CDM predictions to eROSITA observations. Theory parameters are constrained by binary pulsar bounds on present-day deviations from General Relativity.
\n\n
作者在避免引力波标量衰变的一类特定DHOST理论中应用球对称塌缩模型，计算线性增长因子和塌缩时的外推线性密度对比度$\\delta_c(z)$，并采用解析质量函数（可能为Sheth–Tormen或Press–Schechter形式）预测星系团数密度。每个红移仓中的最小晕质量通过将$\\Lambda$CDM模型预测与eROSITA观测结果匹配来确定。理论参数受双脉冲星对当前广义相对论偏离程度的局域天体物理限制所约束。

Result: In viable DHOST models consistent with current bounds, the linear growth of matter perturbations is suppressed on small scales, while $\\delta_c$ increases. This leads to a reduction in predicted cluster counts at high redshift compared to $\\Lambda$CDM, even when normalized to eROSITA at low redshift. The maximum deviation occurs in the highest redshift bin, with the strongest suppression for the largest allowed departure from Einstein gravity.
\n\n

Conclusion: Even under stringent constraints from gravitational-wave and binary pulsar observations, DHOST theories can produce observable imprints on cluster abundances, particularly a suppression at high redshift. However, the robustness of this prediction is limited by theoretical uncertainties in applying the spherical collapse model and analytic mass functions to modified gravity scenarios.
\n\n
即使在引力波和双脉冲星观测的严格约束下，DHOST理论仍可在星系团丰度上留下可观测印记，尤其是高红移处的抑制效应。然而，该预测的可靠性受限于将球对称塌缩模型和解析质量函数应用于修改引力场景时的理论不确定性。

Abstract: We investigate the spherical collapse model and the abundance of galaxy clusters in a class of degenerate higher-order scalar--tensor (DHOST) theories in which gravitational waves do not decay into scalar perturbations and which are consistent with current constraints from gravitational-wave observations. We find that deviations from Einstein gravity can become significant at late times when the background universe is close to the scaling regime during the matter-dominated epoch. These deviations suppress the growth of linear matter perturbations on small scales while increasing the extrapolated linear density contrast at collapse, obtained from the spherical collapse model. Using the analytic mass function, we compute the corresponding cluster number counts. The minimum mass threshold in the mass integration for each redshift bin is determined by matching the predicted number counts in the $Λ$CDM model with those inferred from the eROSITA survey. We find that the cluster abundance reaches its maximum at low redshift bin, and that the number of clusters in the highest redshift bin is suppressed as the deviation from Einstein gravity becomes larger. The parameters of the theory are chosen such that the deviation from Einstein gravity at present is consistent with the local astrophysical bounds from binary pulsar observations. We find that even under such strict constraints, the upper bound on the deviation leads to lower predicted number counts compared with the $Λ$CDM model emulating the eROSITA survey results.
  However, this may be a consequence of the uncertainties in computing the number counts for the DHOST theories using the spherical collapse model and the analytical mass function.

### [3] [Improved recipes for peculiar velocity power spectra using Evolution Mapping](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13948)
*Matteo Esposito,Ariel G. Sánchez,Julien Bel,Andrés N. Ruiz*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: We introduce new, highly accurate fitting functions for the velocity divergence auto- and cross-power spectra ($P_{θθ}(k)$ and $P_{δθ}(k)$), calibrated on high-resolution N-body simulations. By leveraging the Evolution Mapping framework and parametrising in terms of $σ_{12}$—the RMS linear density fluctuation smoothed at 12 Mpc—we achieve percent-level accuracy up to $k \simeq 0.56\,\mathrm{Mpc}^{-1}$ across a broad range of cosmologies, outperforming previous models. We demonstrate that using $σ_{12}$ avoids unphysical Hubble-parameter dependencies inherent in $σ_8$-based parametrisations, offering a more robust foundation for redshift-space distortion modelling.
\n\n
我们提出了新的高精度速度散度自功率谱和互功率谱（$P_{θθ}(k)$ 和 $P_{δθ}(k)$）拟合函数，基于高分辨率 N-body 模拟进行校准。通过采用 Evolution Mapping 框架并以 $σ_{12}$（在 12 Mpc 尺度上平滑的线性密度涨落的均方根）作为参数化变量，我们在宽泛的宇宙学模型范围内实现了高达 $k \simeq 0.56\,\mathrm{Mpc}^{-1}$ 的百分之一量级精度，优于现有模型。我们证明使用 $σ_{12}$ 可避免基于 $σ_8$ 参数化所固有的非物理哈勃参数依赖性，为红移空间畸变建模提供了更稳健的基础。

  <summary>Details</summary>

Moreover, prior models often lack rigorous calibration against converged N-body simulations across diverse cosmologies. The authors aim to construct robust, physically motivated fitting formulas that are both numerically converged and broadly applicable, enabling precise modeling of redshift-space distortions (RSD) in current and future galaxy surveys.
\n\n
现有的速度功率谱拟合函数存在精度有限、适用范围受限以及非物理参数依赖等问题——尤其是当以 $σ_8$ 作为参数时，会引入对哈勃参数 $h$ 的虚假敏感性。此外，以往模型通常缺乏在多种宇宙学背景下对收敛的 N-body 模拟进行严格校准。作者旨在构建既数值收敛又广泛适用的、具有物理动机的稳健拟合公式，以实现对当前及未来星系巡天中红移空间畸变（RSD）的精确建模。

Method: The authors employ the Evolution Mapping framework to derive a physically motivated parametrisation of non-linear velocity statistics in terms of $σ_{12}$, the RMS linear density fluctuation smoothed at 12 Mpc. They calibrate their fitting functions using a suite of high-resolution gravity-only N-body simulations with multiple resolutions to quantify numerical convergence. A conservative patching strategy is applied to mitigate sampling artefacts. The resulting fits for $P_{θθ}(k)$ and $P_{δθ}(k)$ are validated against independent simulations spanning a wide range of cosmological parameters. They also explicitly test the impact of deviations from exact evolution mapping due to varying growth histories and compare parametrisations based on $σ_{12}$ versus $σ_8$.
\n\n
作者采用 Evolution Mapping 框架，以 $σ_{12}$（在 12 Mpc 尺度上平滑的线性密度涨落的均方根）作为参数，对非线性速度统计量进行具有物理动机的参数化。他们利用一套多分辨率的纯引力 N-body 模拟进行拟合函数校准，以量化数值收敛性，并采用保守的拼接策略来减轻采样伪影。所得的 $P_{θθ}(k)$ 和 $P_{δθ}(k)$ 拟合函数在涵盖广泛宇宙学参数的独立模拟中进行了验证。他们还明确检验了因增长历史不同而导致的对精确 Evolution Mapping 关系的偏离影响，并比较了基于 $σ_{12}$ 与 $σ_8$ 的参数化方案。

Result: The new fitting functions achieve percent-level accuracy for both $P_{θθ}(k)$ and $P_{δθ}(k)$ up to $k \simeq 0.56\,\mathrm{Mpc}^{-1}$ across a wide range of $σ_{12}$ values, significantly outperforming existing prescriptions. Validation across diverse cosmologies confirms 1–2% accuracy on robustly measured scales. The study demonstrates that parametrising in terms of $σ_{12}$ eliminates spurious $h$-dependencies present in $σ_8$-based models—even for cosmologies with identical linear clustering. \n\n

\n\n
在 Evolution Mapping 框架内使用 $σ_{12}$ 对非线性速度功率谱进行参数化，可得到稳健、精确且与宇宙学无关的拟合函数。该方法解决了与 $σ_8$ 相关的非物理依赖性，为星系巡天中的红移空间畸变建模提供了可靠工具。研究结果强烈支持将 $σ_{12}$ 作为涉及速度场的非线性结构形成研究中的标准振幅参数。

By applying the Evolution Mapping framework, we revise existing prescriptions to introduce a physically motivated parametrisation in terms of the clustering amplitude $σ_{12}$, the RMS density fluctuation smoothed at $12\,\text{Mpc}$. This approach improves robustness and extends the range of applicability beyond that of previous models. Validation against independent simulations spanning a broad range of cosmological models confirms an accuracy of $1$-$2$ per cent on scales where the measurements are robust, systematically outperforming existing prescriptions. We further assess the impact of deviations from the exact evolution mapping relation induced by differing growth histories. For most cosmologies of practical interest, we find that neglecting these effects introduces only subdominant errors. We show that expressing fitting functions in $h$-dependent units leads to spurious, unphysical dependencies on the Hubble parameter, even for models with identical linear clustering. This provides strong empirical support for parametrising non-linear evolution in terms of $σ_{12}$ rather than $σ_{8}$. Our fitting functions provide a robust description of velocity power spectra, with direct applications to redshift-space distortion modelling in galaxy redshift surveys.

### [4] [KiDS+VIKING-450 cosmology with Bayesian hierarchical model redshift distributions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15078)
*George T. Kyriacou,Arrykrishna Mootoovaloo,Alan F. Heavens,Andrew H. Jaffe,Florent Leclercq,Konrad Kuijken*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: This paper applies a Bayesian Hierarchical Model (BHM) with template-based redshift estimation to KiDS+VIKING-450 weak lensing data, using principal component analysis (PCA) to construct representative spectral templates. By marginalizing over photometric redshift uncertainties, the method yields $S_8 = 0.756 \pm 0.039$, reducing the tension with Planck from $2.3\sigma$ to $1.9\sigma$ in flat $\Lambda$CDM.
\n\n
本文将基于模板的贝叶斯分层模型（Bayesian Hierarchical Model, BHM）应用于KiDS+VIKING-450弱引力透镜数据，利用主成分分析（Principal Component Analysis, PCA）构建具有代表性的光谱模板。通过对测光红移不确定性的边缘化处理，该方法得到 $S_8 = 0.756 \pm 0.039$，在平直 $\Lambda$CDM 模型下将与Planck结果的张力从 $2.3\sigma$ 降低至 $1.9\sigma$。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Accurate tomographic redshift distributions $n(z)$ are essential for cosmic shear analyses because theoretical predictions of the shear power spectrum depend sensitively on the redshift distribution of source galaxies. Traditional point-estimate photometric redshift methods often neglect or inadequately model redshift uncertainties, leading to biased cosmological inference. This work aims to address this by implementing a full probabilistic treatment of redshift uncertainties within a Bayesian framework, thereby improving robustness against photo-$z$ systematics.
\n\n
精确的层析红移分布 $n(z)$ 对宇宙剪切分析至关重要，因为剪切功率谱的理论预测对源星系的红移分布高度敏感。传统的测光红移点估计方法通常忽略或未能充分建模红移不确定性，从而导致宇宙学推断出现偏差。本工作旨在通过在贝叶斯框架内对红移不确定性进行完整的概率处理，以提升对测光红移（photo-$z$）系统误差的鲁棒性。

Method: The authors extend Leistedt et al.'s template-based Bayesian Hierarchical Model to weak lensing. They construct a compact set of galaxy spectral energy distribution (SED) templates via Principal Component Analysis (PCA) from a large superset. Redshift distributions for subsets of $10^5$ galaxies from KiDS+VIKING-450 are inferred by sampling the posterior of the BHM, which simultaneously fits all galaxies and self-consistently accounts for population-level redshift distributions. Cosmological parameters are then inferred by marginalizing over these redshift posteriors within a flat $\Lambda$CDM model.
\n\n
作者将Leistedt等人提出的基于模板的贝叶斯分层模型（BHM）扩展至弱引力透镜应用。他们通过对一个大型光谱能量分布（SED）超集进行主成分分析（PCA），构建了一组紧凑的代表性模板。随后，从KiDS+VIKING-450中选取 $10^5$ 星系子集，通过采样BHM后验分布来推断红移分布——该模型同时拟合所有星系，并自洽地考虑总体红移分布。最终，在平直 $\Lambda$CDM 模型下，通过对这些红移后验进行边缘化来推断宇宙学参数。

The dependence of cosmological inference on the specific galaxy subset chosen ($10^5$ out of the full sample) is found to be subdominant compared to statistical errors.
\n\n

Conclusion: A fully Bayesian treatment of photometric redshift uncertainties using a hierarchical model and PCA-derived templates can significantly impact cosmological parameter inference from weak lensing surveys. By properly marginalizing over redshift distributions, the method mitigates biases and reduces the $S_8$ tension with Planck, demonstrating the importance of rigorous uncertainty quantification in large-scale structure analyses.

Abstract: Tomographic redshift distributions from photometric data are crucial ingredients in cosmic shear analysis, since they are required for the theoretical calculation of the signal based on the redshift distribution of the galaxies where the shear field is sampled. In this paper, we develop as a proof of concept Leistedt et al.'s template-based Bayesian Hierarchical Model framework into an application to weak lensing data by sampling the redshift distributions of the galaxies in the KiDS+VIKING-450 survey. We also use a principal component analysis to provide a set of representative templates drawn from a large superset. For computational tractability, subsets of $10^5$ galaxies are chosen to determine the redshift distributions, and we test the sensitivity of the cosmological inference to the subset chosen, finding it to be subdominant compared to the statistical error. We marginalise over the inferred redshift distributions and find that the Bayesian method increases the clustering parameter compared with previous studies, alleviating the $S_8$ tension with Planck, where $S_{8}\equivσ_{8}\sqrt{Ω_{\tm{m}}/0.3}=0.756\pm 0.039$, assuming flat $Λ$CDM. The tension with Planck for this survey is reduced from $2.3σ$ to $1.9σ$. We also infer a value for the matter density, $Ω_{\tm{m}}=0.31\pm 0.10$.
