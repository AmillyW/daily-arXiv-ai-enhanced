### [1] [Neural network enhanced Bayesian global analysis of relativistic heavy ion collisions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26413)
*Jussi Auvinen,Kari J. Eskola,Henry Hirvonen,Harri Niemi*

Main category: hep-ph

TL;DR: This paper introduces a deep convolutional neural network (CNN)-enhanced Bayesian global analysis for bulk observables in heavy-ion collisions, replacing computationally intensive hydrodynamical simulations with CNNs that directly predict observables from initial energy density profiles. The method enables efficient constraint of 15 free parameters (13 for QCD matter + 2 for initial state) using RHIC and LHC collision data, yielding constraints on shear viscosity $\eta/s$ and bulk viscosity $\zeta/s$.

本文提出了一种基于深度卷积神经网络（CNN）的贝叶斯全局分析方法，用于重离子碰撞中的整体可观测量。该方法用CNN替代计算密集型的流体动力学模拟，直接从初始能量密度分布预测可观测量。该方法能够高效约束15个自由参数（13个用于QCD物质+2个用于初始状态），利用RHIC和LHC碰撞数据，得到剪切粘滞系数$\eta/s$和体积粘滞系数$\zeta/s$的约束。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: To overcome computational limitations in traditional Bayesian global analyses of heavy-ion collisions, which require extensive hydrodynamical simulations. The authors aim to include statistics-hungry flow observables (e.g., $v_4$, $NSC(4,2)$) by replacing hydrodynamical simulations with CNNs that predict bulk observables from initial conditions.

为克服传统重离子碰撞贝叶斯全局分析中的计算限制（传统方法需要大量流体动力学模拟），作者旨在通过用CNN替代流体动力学模拟（从初始条件直接预测整体可观测量）来包含更多统计需求高的流观测量（例如$v_4$和$NSC(4,2)$）。

Method: A deep convolutional neural network (CNN) takes event-by-event initial energy density profiles (from EKRT model) as input and outputs bulk observables, accounting for 13 QCD-matter parameters and 2 initial-state parameters. The CNN output trains Gaussian process emulators for centrality-averaged observables and uncertainties, replacing full hydrodynamical simulations to reduce computational cost.

深度卷积神经网络（CNN）以事件-事件初始能量密度分布（来自EKRT模型）为输入，输出整体可观测量，同时考虑13个QCD物质参数和2个初始状态参数。CNN输出用于训练高斯过程模拟器，以获得中心度平均可观测量及其不确定性，从而替代完整流体动力学模拟以降低计算成本。

Result: Shear viscosity over entropy density $\eta/s$ shows a minimum-value plateau at $150 \lesssim T \lesssim 230$ MeV with $0.12 \lesssim (\eta/s)_{\mathrm{min}} \lesssim 0.18$. Bulk viscous coefficient $\zeta/s$ is non-zero at $200 \lesssim T \lesssim 300$ MeV. The Knudsen number at freeze-out is $0.8-2.3$ and the mean free path to system size ratio is $0.3-1.2$.

剪切粘滞系数与熵密度之比$\eta/s$在$150 \lesssim T \lesssim 230$ MeV范围内存在最小值平台，其中$0.12 \lesssim (\eta/s)_{\mathrm{min}} \lesssim 0.18$。体积粘滞系数$\zeta/s$在$200 \lesssim T \lesssim 300$ MeV范围内非零。冻结时的Knudsen数为$0.8-2.3$，平均自由程与系统尺寸比值为$0.3-1.2$。

Conclusion: The method demonstrates feasibility of CNN-enhanced Bayesian global analysis for heavy-ion collisions, enabling efficient constraint of QCD matter properties. Results indicate a specific $\eta/s$ plateau and non-zero $\zeta/s$ in the QCD phase transition region, with freeze-out occurring at the expected hydrodynamic limit (Knudsen number $0.8-2.3$).

该方法证明了在重离子碰撞中使用CNN增强的贝叶斯全局分析的可行性，能够高效约束QCD物质性质。结果表明，在QCD相变区域存在特定的$\eta/s$平台和非零的$\zeta/s$，冻结过程发生在预期的流体动力学极限（Knudsen数$0.8-2.3$）。

Abstract: We introduce a novel deep convolutional neural network (NN) -enhanced Bayesian global analysis of bulk observables in highest-energy heavy-ion collisions, using relativistic 2+1 D second-order viscous hydrodynamics with a dynamical freeze-out, and with perturbative QCD and saturation -based initial conditions from the event-by-event EKRT-model. Our analysis has 13+2 free parameters for the QCD-matter properties + initial state, which are constrained by the experimental data from $\sqrt{s_{NN}}=200$ GeV Au+Au collisions at RHIC and $2.76$ TeV Pb+Pb, $5.02$ TeV Pb+Pb, and $5.44$ TeV Xe+Xe collisions at the LHC. We replace the computationally demanding hydrodynamical simulations by NNs, which predict bulk observables directly from the initial energy density profiles, event-by-event, and account for the QCD-matter properties. With the NN output, we train the Gaussian process emulators for obtaining centrality-class averaged observables and their uncertainties. The NNs reduce the computing time significantly, enabling us to include also statistics-hungry flow observables like $v_4$ and the normalized symmetric cumulant $NSC(4,2)$ in the analysis. In this paper, we demonstrate the feasibility of the NN based Bayesian global analysis. We find the data favoring a specific shear viscosity $η/s$ with a minimum-value plateau at temperatures $150\lesssim T \lesssim 230$ MeV, with $0.12 \lesssim (η/s)_{\mathrm{min}} \lesssim 0.18$. The bulk viscous coefficient $ζ/s$ is non-zero at $200\lesssim T \lesssim 300$ MeV. The Knudsen number at the freeze-out is $0.8-2.3$, while the ratio of the mean free path to the system size at freeze-out is in the range $0.3-1.2$, implying that the freeze-out indeed happens at the expected limit of the applicability of hydrodynamics.

### [2] [Sensitivity to new physics: single-Higgs couplings vs. the trilinear Higgs coupling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26633)
*Henning Bahl,Johannes Braathen,Martin Gabelmann,Sven Heinemeyer,Kateryna Radchenko Serdula,Alain Verduras Schaeidt,Georg Weiglein*

Main category: hep-ph

TL;DR: The trilinear Higgs self-coupling (λ₃) serves as a critical probe for Higgs potential structure and electroweak phase transition. This paper identifies BSM scenarios where λ₃ deviates significantly from the SM value while other Higgs observables remain SM-like, highlighting the necessity of precise measurements at future Higgs factories.

trilinear Higgs self-coupling (λ₃) 是探测希格斯势结构和电弱相变的关键探针。本文识别出在BSM模型中λ₃与标准模型值显著偏离，而其他希格斯可观测量仍接近标准模型的情形，强调了未来希格斯工厂进行精确测量的必要性.


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: The trilinear Higgs coupling (λ₃) provides unique sensitivity to the Higgs potential's structure and electroweak phase transition dynamics. Current experimental constraints on λ₃ are insufficient, and its precise measurement offers a complementary test of BSM physics, particularly in extended Higgs sectors where other Higgs observables (e.g., h→γγ) remain SM-consistent.

trilinear Higgs coupling (λ₃) 对希格斯势结构和电弱相变动力学具有独特敏感性。当前对λ₃的实验约束不足，其精确测量为BSM物理提供了互补性检验，特别是在其他希格斯可观测量（如h→γγ）仍与标准模型一致的扩展希格斯扇区中.

Method: The study employs precise analytical calculations and automated BSM model implementations to compute the trilinear Higgs coupling (λ₃) in extended Higgs sectors. Key methods include renormalization group (RG) evolution of Higgs potential parameters and matching to effective field theory (EFT) frameworks to isolate deviations from the SM value.

该研究采用精确解析计算和自动化BSM模型实现，计算扩展希格斯扇区中的trilinear Higgs coupling (λ₃)。关键方法包括希格斯势参数的重整化群（RG）演化以及与有效场论（EFT）框架的匹配，以分离与标准模型值的偏差.

Result: In specific BSM models (e.g., 2HDM, Higgs inflation), significant deviations in $ \lambda_3 $ are predicted: $ \lambda_3 = \lambda_{\text{SM}} (1 + \delta) $ with $ |\delta| \gtrsim 10\% $, while other Higgs observables (e.g., $ \Gamma(h \to \gamma\gamma) $, $ m_h $) remain within 1\sigma of SM expectations. This demonstrates $ \lambda_3 $ as a unique probe for BSM physics.

在特定BSM模型（如2HDM、希格斯暴胀）中，预测$ \lambda_3 $存在显著偏差：$ \lambda_3 = \lambda_{\text{SM}} (1 + \delta) $，其中$ |\delta| \gtrsim 10\% $，而其他希格斯可观测量（如$ \Gamma(h \to \gamma\gamma) $、$ m_h $）仍处于标准模型期望值的1\sigma范围内。这表明$ \lambda_3 $是探测BSM物理的独特探针.

Conclusion: A precise measurement of the trilinear Higgs self-coupling (λ₃) at future Higgs factories is indispensable for testing BSM physics, as it can reveal deviations unobservable through other Higgs observables. This underscores the critical role of λ₃ as a complementary probe to standard Higgs measurements.

未来希格斯工厂对trilinear Higgs self-coupling (λ₃)的精确测量对于检验BSM物理不可或缺，因其能揭示其他希格斯可观测量无法观测到的偏差。这凸显了$ \lambda_3 $作为标准希格斯测量的互补性探针的关键作用.

Abstract: The trilinear Higgs self-coupling provides a unique probe of the structure of the Higgs potential and of the nature of the electroweak phase transition, and constitutes a key target for future collider experiments. Recent studies have shown that confronting theoretical predictions for the trilinear Higgs coupling with current experimental bounds offers a powerful and complementary way to test effects of physics beyond the Standard Model (BSM), in particular those arising from extended Higgs sectors. Meanwhile, substantial progress has been achieved in the precise calculation and automation of the trilinear Higgs coupling in a wide class of BSM models. This contribution discusses several BSM scenarios, compatible with existing constraints, in which sizeable deviations in the trilinear Higgs coupling w.r.t. the Standard Model (SM) value are predicted, while other Higgs observables remain close to their SM expectations and are therefore difficult to probe experimentally. These results highlight the strong physics motivation for a precise measurement of the trilinear Higgs coupling at a future Higgs factory.

### [3] [Primordial Black Hole interpretation of the sub-solar merger event S251112cm](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25795)
*Md Riajul Haque,Fabio Iocco,Luca Visinelli*

Main category: astro-ph.CO

TL;DR: This paper evaluates the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA event S251112cm as a potential merger of primordial black holes (PBHs) with sub-solar masses. Using analytic merger rate calculations and observational constraints, it demonstrates that a PBH interpretation is viable within current bounds, with detection probability reaching $\sim 1$ for $M_{\rm PBH} \sim 0.5$-$1\,M_\odot$ in relaxed scenarios.

本文评估LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA事件S251112cm为亚太阳质量原初黑洞(PBHs)并合的潜在解释。通过解析并合率计算和观测限制，证明在当前约束下PBH解释是可行的，在放宽情景下$M_{\rm PBH} \sim 0.5$-$1\,M_\odot$时探测概率可达$\sim 1$.


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: The observed sub-solar mass compact object in S251112cm cannot be explained by standard stellar evolution, motivating the investigation of non-standard formation channels. Primordial black holes (PBHs), formed from early Universe density fluctuations, provide a well-motivated candidate to explain such events.

S251112cm中观测到的亚太阳质量致密天体无法通过标准恒星演化解释，促使研究非标准形成通道。由早期宇宙密度涨落形成的原初黑洞(PBHs)是解释此类事件的合理候选者。

Method: The study combines analytic estimates of the PBH merger rate with current constraints on PBH abundance and LVK sensitivity. It computes the probability of observing S251112cm as a PBH merger using the formula $P = \frac{R_{\rm obs}}{R_{\rm total}}$, where $R_{\rm obs}$ is the observed rate and $R_{\rm total}$ is the total predicted rate.

该研究将PBH并合率的解析估计与当前PBH丰度约束及LVK灵敏度相结合。通过公式$P = \frac{R_{\rm obs}}{R_{\rm total}}$计算将S251112cm解释为PBH并合的观测概率，其中$R_{\rm obs}$为观测率，$R_{\rm total}$为总预测率。

Result: The detection probability reaches unity for $M_{\rm PBH} \sim 0.5$-$1\,M_\odot$ in relaxed constraint scenarios, while remaining $\sim \mathcal{O}(0.5)$ in more conservative cases. The results indicate the viability of a PBH interpretation within current observational bounds.

在放宽约束情景下，$M_{\rm PBH} \sim 0.5$-$1\,M_\odot$时探测概率达到1，而在更保守情景下仍保持$\sim \mathcal{O}(0.5)$。结果表明在当前观测约束下PBH解释是可行的。

Conclusion: While the PBH interpretation of S251112cm is viable, astrophysical uncertainties prevent conclusive determination of the event's nature. The analysis underscores the potential of sub-solar gravitational wave events to probe PBHs and their dark matter contribution.

虽然S251112cm的PBH解释是可行的，但天体物理不确定性阻碍了对事件本质的确定性判断。该分析强调了亚太阳质量引力波事件探测PBHs及其暗物质贡献的潜力。

Abstract: The LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA (LVK) candidate event S251112cm suggests the presence of at least one compact object with sub-solar masses. Since such objects cannot be produced through standard stellar evolution, this observation provides a potential indication of non-standard formation channels. Primordial black holes (PBHs), formed from the collapse of primordial density fluctuations in the early Universe, are a well-motivated candidate. We investigate the interpretation of S251112cm as the merger of two PBHs with masses in the range 0.1-1$\,M_\odot$. Combining analytic estimates of the PBH merger rate with current observational constraints on their abundance and the sensitivity of LVK searches, we compute the probability of observing such an event. Within a relaxed constraint scenario, the probability reaches unity in the range $M_{\rm PBH} \sim 0.5$-$1\,M_\odot$, while it remains sizable, $\sim \mathcal{O}(0.5)$, in more conservative scenarios and at lower masses. Our results show that a PBH interpretation of S251112cm is viable within current bounds. Owing to the dependence of our results on astrophysical uncertainties, such as those affecting the constraints on the abundance of PBHs, they cannot be regarded as conclusive with respect to the nature of the detected event. At the same time, our analysis highlights the potential of sub-solar gravitational wave events as a probe of PBHs and their contribution to dark matter.

### [4] [Electromagnetic radiation mediated by topological surface states](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26495)
*M. Ibarra-Meneses,A. Martín-Ruiz*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: We study classical electromagnetic radiation modifications at topological-insulator interfaces using axion electrodynamics, deriving perturbative corrections to Liénard-Wiechert potentials via a piecewise constant $θ$-term. The key results include azimuthal modulations in antenna radiation and a universal attenuation factor $1 - (\sigma_{\mathrm{Hall}} / 2\varepsilon v)^2$ for bremsstrahlung, demonstrating topological surface states' role in mediating classical radiation.

本研究基于axion electrodynamics框架，分析拓扑绝缘体界面处的经典电磁辐射修正。通过分段常数$θ$-项的微扰处理，推导Liénard-Wiechert势的解析修正。关键结果包括天线辐射的方位调制及轫致辐射的普适衰减因子$1 - (\sigma_{\mathrm{Hall}} / 2\varepsilon v)^2$，揭示拓扑表面态对经典辐射的介导作用.


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: To investigate how topological surface states modify classical electromagnetic radiation through axion electrodynamics, focusing on the magnetoelectric response encoded by a piecewise constant $θ$-term at the interface between topological and trivial insulators.

探究拓扑表面态如何通过axion electrodynamics修改经典电磁辐射，重点分析拓扑与平凡绝缘体界面处分段常数$θ$-项所编码的磁电响应。

Method: Perturbative expansion of the axion coupling in the $θ$-term within axion electrodynamics, using $δθ = θ_{\text{top}} - θ_{\text{triv}}$ as the expansion parameter to derive analytical corrections to Liénard-Wiechert potentials.

在axion electrodynamics框架下对$θ$-项中的轴子耦合进行微扰展开，以$δθ = θ_{\text{top}} - θ_{\text{triv}}$为展开参数推导Liénard-Wiechert势的解析修正。

Result: The far-zone radiation fields exhibit analytical corrections $δA^μ$ to the standard Liénard-Wiechert potentials, with a universal attenuation factor $1 - (\sigma_{\mathrm{Hall}} / 2\varepsilon v)^2$ for bremsstrahlung, where $\sigma_{\mathrm{Hall}}$ is the surface Hall conductivity.

远区辐射场表现出对标准Liénard-Wiechert势的解析修正$δA^μ$，轫致辐射存在普适衰减因子$1 - (\sigma_{\mathrm{Hall}} / 2\varepsilon v)^2$，其中$\sigma_{\mathrm{Hall}}$为表面霍尔电导率。

Conclusion: This work establishes a theoretical link between axion electrodynamics, topological phases, and spatially varying couplings, demonstrating that topological surface states mediate measurable modifications to classical radiation through interference with image magnetic monopoles.

本工作建立了axion electrodynamics、拓扑相与空间变化耦合之间的理论联系，证明拓扑表面态通过与镜像磁单极子的干涉介导经典辐射的可测量修正。

Abstract: We study electromagnetic radiation from classical sources near a planar interface separating a topological and a trivial insulator, modeled within axion electrodynamics. The system features a piecewise constant $θ$-term that encodes the magnetoelectric response of topological surface states. Treating this coupling perturbatively, we derive analytical corrections to the standard Liénard-Wiechert potentials and obtain modified radiation fields in the far zone. As applications, we analyze the emission from linear antennas and the bremsstrahlung radiation of accelerated charges near the interface. For antennas, the surface Hall response breaks axial symmetry and produces azimuthal modulations that grow with the electrical length, leading to distinct scaling behaviors in the total and angular radiated power. {For accelerated charges, the emitted intensity is uniformly reduced by a factor $1 - (σ_{\mathrm{Hall}} / 2εv)^2$, which we interpret as a process-specific attenuation of the radiative strength due to interference with its image magnetic monopole inside the topological medium.} These results reveal how topological surface states mediate measurable modifications to classical radiation, establishing a link between axion electrodynamics, topological phases, and field theories with spatially varying couplings.
