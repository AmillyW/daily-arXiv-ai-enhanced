### [1] [The Hodograph Transform Between Thermodynamics and Relativity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.28039)
*Leonid Polterovich*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: In the contact-geometric framework of general relativity, the sky of an event—defined as the set of incoming light rays—is modeled as a Legendrian submanifold in the spherical cotangent bundle of a Cauchy hypersurface. By selecting the Minkowski hyperboloid as the hypersurface, a hyperbolic hodograph transform maps this geometry to a thermodynamic phase space. For a uniformly accelerating observer, the generating functions of evolving skies, upon rescaling, correspond to reduced free energies influenced by the relativistic Doppler effect. This yields an effective temperature proportional to acceleration—matching the Unruh scaling but differing in the numerical coefficient.

在广义相对论的接触几何框架中，事件的“天幕”（即所有入射光子的集合）被建模为柯西超曲面球余切丛中的勒让德子流形。当选择闵可夫斯基双曲面作为超曲面时，双曲版本的焦散变换将此几何映射到热力学相空间。对于均匀加速观测者，其天幕演化的生成函数经适当缩放后，对应于受相对论多普勒效应支配的约化自由能。由此导出的有效温度与加速度成正比——符合安鲁效应的标度律，但数值系数不同。

Motivation: The paper aims to explore deep connections between geometric structures in general relativity and thermodynamics, particularly through the lens of contact geometry. By interpreting the sky of an event as a Legendrian submanifold, it seeks to uncover a thermodynamic interpretation of spacetime phenomena, especially for accelerated observers, potentially shedding light on analogies like the Unruh effect without relying on quantum field theory.

本文旨在通过接触几何的视角，探索广义相对论中几何结构与热力学之间的深层联系。通过将事件的“天幕”视为勒让德子流形，试图揭示时空现象（特别是加速观测者情形）的热力学解释，从而在不依赖量子场论的情况下，为类似安鲁效应的现象提供类比理解。

Method: The authors employ the contact-geometric formulation of general relativity, where light ray congruences are described via Legendrian submanifolds in the spherical cotangent bundle $ T^*\Sigma \setminus 0 / \mathbb{R}^+ $ of a Cauchy surface $\Sigma$. Choosing $\Sigma$ as the Minkowski hyperboloid $ H^3 $, they apply a hyperbolic version of the hodograph transform to identify this bundle with a thermodynamic phase space (coordinates: intensive and extensive variables). For a uniformly accelerating observer starting on $ H^3 $, they compute the generating functions of evolving skies and rescale them to interpret as reduced free energies. The associated thermodynamic quantities—such as effective temperature—are derived from these functions using standard thermodynamic relations.

作者采用广义相对论的接触几何表述，其中光线汇通过柯西面$\Sigma$的球余切丛$ T^*\Sigma \setminus 0 / \mathbb{R}^+ $中的勒让德子流形描述。选择$\Sigma$为闵可夫斯基双曲面$ H^3 $，应用双曲版本的焦散变换，将此丛识别为热力学相空间（坐标对应强度量与广延量）。对于从$ H^3 $出发的均匀加速观测者，计算其演化天幕的生成函数，并进行缩放以解释为约化自由能。利用标准热力学关系，从这些函数导出相应的热力学量（如有效温度）。

Result: The generating functions of the skies, after rescaling, are shown to behave as reduced free energies of equilibrium thermodynamic systems, with the relativistic Doppler shift acting as the governing physical mechanism. An effective temperature $ T_\text{eff} $ is extracted, which scales linearly with the observer's proper acceleration $ a $: $ T_\text{eff} \propto a $. While this matches the scaling predicted by the Unruh effect ($ T_\text{Unruh} = \frac{\hbar a}{2\pi c k_B} $), the numerical prefactor differs from the standard Unruh value, indicating a classical geometric origin rather than a quantum one.

天幕的生成函数经缩放后表现为平衡态热力学系统的约化自由能，相对论多普勒频移是主导物理机制。提取的有效温度$ T_\text{eff} $与观测者的固有加速度$ a $线性相关：$ T_\text{eff} \propto a $。虽然这与安鲁效应的标度律（$ T_\text{Unruh} = \frac{\hbar a}{2\pi c k_B} $）一致，但数值因子不同，表明其来源于经典几何而非量子效应。

Conclusion: The study establishes a novel correspondence between the evolution of light ray congruences (skies) in curved spacetime and equilibrium thermodynamics, mediated by contact geometry and the hyperbolic hodograph transform. The emergence of an acceleration-dependent effective temperature, while reminiscent of the Unruh effect, arises purely from classical geometric considerations, suggesting that key features of 'thermodynamic gravity' may be encoded in spacetime geometry itself, independent of quantum field theory in curved spacetime.

本研究建立了弯曲时空中光线汇（天幕）演化与平衡态热力学之间的新对应关系，该关系由接触几何和双曲焦散变换所中介。出现的加速度依赖性有效温度虽类似于安鲁效应，却完全源于经典几何考量，表明“热力学引力”的关键特征可能已编码于时空几何本身，而无需依赖弯曲时空中的量子场论。

Abstract: In the contact-geometric approach to general relativity, the sky of an event - namely, the set of all incoming light rays - forms a Legendrian submanifold of the spherical cotangent bundle of a Cauchy hypersurface. When the hypersurface is chosen to be the Minkowski hyperboloid, a hyperbolic version of the hodograph transform identifies this bundle with a thermodynamic phase space. We consider a uniformly accelerating observer starting on the hyperboloid and study the evolution of its skies. We show that the associated generating functions, after a suitable rescaling, admit a natural interpretation as reduced free energies of equilibrium thermodynamic systems governed by the relativistic Doppler effect. From this data, we extract an effective temperature that is proportional to the acceleration, in agreement with the scaling of the Unruh effect, although the numerical constant differs from the Unruh value.

### [2] [Universality in Relativistic Spinning Particle Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.27353)
*Joon-Hwi Kim,Sangmin Lee*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: We prove the equivalence of four massive spinning particle models (vector oscillator, spinor oscillator, spherical top, and massive twistor) in 4D when coupled to electromagnetism or gravity, within the spin-magnitude-preserving sector. The Bargmann-Michel-Telegdi and quadrupolar Mathisson-Papapetrou-Dixon equations are derived universally, and a consistent interacting spherical top model is constructed with attention to spin gauge invariance. The framework supports higher spin multipoles and has applications in black hole physics and post-Newtonian/post-Minkowskian expansions.

我们证明了四种有质量自旋粒子模型（矢量振子、旋量振子、球形陀螺和有质量旋量）在四维时空中与电磁场或引力场耦合时，在保持自旋模长的条件下完全等价。以模型无关的方式推导出了Bargmann-Michel-Telegdi方程和四极Mathisson-Papapetrou-Dixon方程，并严格构建了具有自旋规范不变性的相互作用球形陀螺模型。该普适框架可纳入更高阶自旋多极相互作用，适用于黑洞物理、守恒荷以及后牛顿/后闵可夫斯基展开。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Spinning particle models are essential for describing relativistic dynamics of rotating bodies in classical and quantum regimes, especially in gravitational and electromagnetic fields. However, multiple formulations exist in the literature—such as the vector oscillator, spinor oscillator, spherical top, and massive twistor—leading to ambiguity about their physical equivalence. Clarifying this equivalence, particularly under interactions preserving spin magnitude, is crucial for robust modeling in high-precision contexts like black hole binaries and effective field theories.

自旋粒子模型对于描述相对论性旋转体在经典与量子区域的动力学至关重要，尤其是在引力场和电磁场中。然而，文献中存在多种表述形式——如矢量振子、旋量振子、球形陀螺和有质量旋量——导致其物理等价性不明确。澄清这些模型之间的等价性，特别是在保持自旋模长的相互作用下的等价性，对于黑洞双星系统和有效场论等高精度建模具有重要意义。

Method: We analyze the phase space structure and symmetries of four spinning particle models in four dimensions: the vector oscillator, spinor oscillator, spherical top, and massive twistor. By restricting to the spin-magnitude-preserving sector, we construct canonical transformations mapping one model to another, establishing their classical equivalence. Using Hamiltonian methods and spin gauge symmetry, we derive the Bargmann-Michel-Telegdi (BMT) equation for electrodynamics and the quadrupolar Mathisson-Papapetrou-Dixon (QMPD) equation for gravity in a model-independent way. For the spherical top, we formulate the interacting theory via a minimal coupling scheme that respects spin gauge invariance, ensuring consistency.

我们分析了四种四维自旋粒子模型（矢量振子、旋量振子、球形陀螺、有质量旋量）的相空间结构与对称性。通过限制在自旋模长保持的扇区，构造了将一个模型映射到另一个模型的正则变换，从而建立它们的经典等价性。利用哈密顿方法和自旋规范对称性，以模型无关的方式推导出电磁学中的Bargmann-Michel-Telegdi (BMT) 方程和引力中的四极Mathisson-Papapetrou-Dixon (QMPD) 方程。对于球形陀螺模型，我们通过尊重自旋规范不变性的最小耦合方案来构建其相互作用理论，确保一致性。

Result: The four models are proven to be classically equivalent in both free and interacting theories when restricted to the spin-magnitude-preserving sector. The BMT and QMPD equations are derived universally without reference to a specific model. A consistent interacting version of the spherical top is constructed with explicit spin gauge invariance. The framework naturally accommodates higher-spin multipole couplings, enabling systematic extensions. Applications include black hole dynamics, conserved charges via Noether's theorem, and compatibility with post-Newtonian and post-Minkowskian perturbative frameworks.

四种模型在自由理论和相互作用理论中被证明在自旋模长保持扇区内是经典等价的。BMT方程和QMPD方程以不依赖于具体模型的方式被普遍推导出来。构建了一个具明确自旋规范不变性的球形陀螺相互作用版本。该框架自然容纳高阶自旋多极耦合，允许系统性扩展。应用包括黑洞动力学、通过Noether定理得到的守恒荷，以及与后牛顿和后闵可夫斯基微扰框架的兼容性。

Conclusion: A universal framework for massive spinning particles in four dimensions is established, unifying various seemingly distinct models through canonical equivalence in the spin-magnitude-preserving sector. This provides a solid foundation for studying spin effects in strong-field gravity and precision astrophysics, including black hole binaries and gravitational wave templates. The preservation of spin gauge symmetry in interactions ensures consistency and opens the door to incorporating higher-order multipole moments in a controlled manner.

我们建立了四维有质量自旋粒子的普适框架，通过在自旋模长保持扇区内的正则等价性统一了多种看似不同的模型。这为研究强引力场中的自旋效应和高精度天体物理（包括黑洞双星和引力波模板）提供了坚实基础。相互作用中自旋规范对称性的保持确保了理论一致性，并为可控地引入高阶多极矩打开了通道。

Abstract: We establish an equivalence between massive spinning particle models in four spacetime dimensions coupled to electromagnetism or gravity, within the spin-magnitude-preserving sector. Four representative models in the literature are shown to describe exactly the same physics in their free and interacting theories: vector oscillator, spinor oscillator, spherical top, and massive twistor. The Bargmann-Michel-Telegdi (BMT) and quadrupolar Mathisson-Papapetrou-Dixon (QMPD) equations are derived in a model-independent fashion. This universal framework allows for incorporating higher spin multipole interactions as well. We establish the rigorous construction of the interacting theory of the spherical top model with emphasis on spin gauge invariance. Applications to black hole physics, conserved charges, and post-Newtonian or post-Minkowskian frameworks are discussed.
