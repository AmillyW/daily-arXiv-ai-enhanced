### [1] [Dymnikova Black Holes in Unimodular Gravity: Maxwell Sources and Vacuum Contributions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15255)
*G. Alencar,V. H. U. Borralho*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: This paper reinterprets the Dymnikova regular black hole in the context of unimodular gravity, showing that its regular geometry can arise either from nonlinear electrodynamics or from standard Maxwell electrodynamics coupled to a dynamically generated, radial-dependent cosmological term $Λ(r)$ induced by a mild violation of energy–momentum conservation. The resulting electric field is globally regular and corresponds to a localized charge with zero total asymptotic charge.
\n\n
本文在单模引力（unimodular gravity）框架下重新诠释了Dymnikova正则黑洞，表明其正则几何既可以源于非线性电动力学，也可以由标准Maxwell电动力学与一个由能量-动量张量轻微破坏协变守恒所动态生成的径向依赖宇宙学项 $Λ(r)$ 耦合产生。所得电场处处正则，对应于一个局域电荷分布，其渐近总电荷为零。

Motivation: The motivation stems from the desire to understand how regular black hole geometries—free of curvature singularities—can emerge from physically motivated modifications of general relativity and matter sectors. Unimodular gravity offers a natural setting where the cosmological constant becomes dynamical, and controlled violations of $\
abla_\\mu T^{\\mu\
u} = 0$ allow for an effective vacuum energy that varies with spacetime position. This provides a novel mechanism to regularize black hole interiors without introducing exotic matter fields ad hoc.

动机源于理解无曲率奇点的正则黑洞几何如何从广义相对论及其物质部分的物理合理修改中自然产生。单模引力提供了一个天然框架，在其中宇宙学常数变为动力学量，且对 $\

Method: The authors first recast the Dymnikova metric as a solution sourced by nonlinear electrodynamics, extracting the corresponding electric and magnetic field components. Then, they reformulate the same geometry within unimodular gravity, where the trace-free Einstein equations are used, and the cosmological term arises as an integration function $Λ(r)$. By coupling standard Maxwell electrodynamics to this framework and allowing $\
abla_\\mu T^{\\mu\
u} \
eq 0$, they solve for a regular electric field consistent with the spacetime geometry and derive the associated localized charge density via Gauss’s law.

Result: The Dymnikova regular black hole can be equivalently described either by nonlinear electrodynamics in general relativity or by standard Maxwell theory in unimodular gravity with a radial-dependent cosmological function $Λ(r)$. In the latter case, the electric field remains finite everywhere, and the total charge vanishes at spatial infinity, implying the spacetime is not asymptotically Reissner–Nordström-like. The effective vacuum sector, encoded in $Λ(r)$, plays a crucial role in smoothing the central singularity.
\n\n
Dymnikova正则黑洞既可以由广义相对论中的非线性电动力学描述，也可以等价地由单模引力中具有径向依赖宇宙学函数 $Λ(r)$ 的标准Maxwell理论描述。在后一种情形中，电场处处有限，且总电荷在空间无穷远处为零，表明该时空并非渐近Reissner–Nordström型。编码于 $Λ(r)$ 中的有效真空部分在平滑中心奇点中起关键作用。

Conclusion: The work demonstrates that unimodular gravity provides a viable and physically transparent mechanism to generate regular black hole solutions using conventional matter fields. The dynamic cosmological term $Λ(r)$, emerging from a relaxed conservation law, effectively mimics the role of nonlinear electrodynamics, offering a new perspective on singularity resolution through modifications of gravitational dynamics rather than exotic matter content.
\n\n
本研究表明，单模引力通过常规物质场即可提供一种可行且物理清晰的机制来生成正则黑洞解。由放宽的守恒律所引出的动力学宇宙学项 $Λ(r)$ 有效模拟了非线性电动力学的作用，为通过修改引力动力学而非引入奇异物质来解决奇点问题提供了新视角。

Abstract: In this work, we investigate the Dymnikova regular black hole within the framework of unimodular gravity, emphasizing the role of the effective vacuum sector in the regularization of the geometry. By allowing a controlled violation of the covariant conservation of the energy--momentum tensor, the cosmological contribution emerges dynamically as a radial-dependent function, $Λ=Λ(r)$. We first reinterpret the Dymnikova spacetime as a charged configuration supported by nonlinear electrodynamics and derive the corresponding electric and magnetic sources. Subsequently, we demonstrate that the same geometry can be consistently generated by standard Maxwell electrodynamics in unimodular gravity. In this construction, the resulting electric field is everywhere regular and corresponds to a localized charge distribution with vanishing asymptotic charge, indicating that the spacetime does not behave as an asymptotically charged object.

### [2] [Polarization Analysis of Ringdown Signals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15271)
*Nicole Khusid,Will Farr,Max Isi*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: The paper investigates how equatorial reflection symmetry in binary black hole mergers constrains the polarization degrees of freedom in ringdown gravitational waves, enabling inclination inference from circular polarization measurements using only LIGO data for non-precessing systems like GW150914. However, applying this constrained model to precessing systems such as GW190521 introduces significant biases in mode parameter estimation due to mismatched polarization structure.
\n\n
本文研究了双黑洞并合过程中赤道反射对称性如何约束铃宕（ringdown）引力波的偏振自由度，使得仅用LIGO数据即可通过对圆偏振度的测量推断非进动系统（如GW150914）的倾角。然而，将该受限偏振模型应用于进动系统（如GW190521）时，会因偏振结构不匹配而在模式参数估计中引入显著偏差。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: The motivation stems from the need to accurately model ringdown gravitational wave signals to extract physical parameters of the remnant Kerr black hole. For non-precessing binaries with spins (anti)aligned to orbital angular momentum, equatorial reflection symmetry reduces the four polarization degrees of freedom per quasi-normal mode to two, linking amplitudes and phases via the source inclination angle. Understanding whether this symmetry-based simplification remains valid—and what biases arise when it is misapplied—is crucial for unbiased parameter estimation as detector sensitivity improves.
\n\n
动机源于精确建模铃宕引力波信号以提取残余Kerr黑洞物理参数的需求。对于自旋与轨道角动量（反）对齐的非进动双星系统，赤道反射对称性将每个准正规模（quasi-normal mode）的四个偏振自由度减少为两个，并通过源倾角将振幅与相位关联起来。随着探测器灵敏度提升，理解这种基于对称性的简化是否仍然有效，以及误用时会产生何种偏差，对无偏参数估计至关重要。

Method: The authors construct a constrained ringdown waveform model that enforces equatorial reflection symmetry, thereby fixing the relationship between the plus ($h_+$) and cross ($h_\\times$) polarization amplitudes and phases for each quasi-normal mode based on the inclination angle $\\iota$. They fit this model to real events (GW150914, non-precessing; GW190521, precessing) and simulated injections resembling GW190521. They compare results against an arbitrary-polarization model with no symmetry constraints, quantifying biases in inferred mode frequencies, damping times, and amplitudes.
\n\n
作者构建了一个强制赤道反射对称性的受限铃宕波形模型，从而根据倾角 $\\iota$ 固定每个准正规模的“+”偏振（$h_+$）与“×”偏振（$h_\\times$）的振幅和相位之间的关系。他们将该模型拟合到真实事件（非进动的GW150914和进动的GW190521）以及类似GW190521的模拟注入信号上，并与无对称性约束的任意偏振模型进行比较，量化在推断模式频率、阻尼时间和振幅中的偏差。

Result: For GW150914, the constrained model successfully measures the degree of circular polarization and handedness using only two LIGO detectors, allowing inclination inference solely from the ringdown. In contrast, when applied to precessing systems like GW190521, the same model fails to capture the true polarization structure, leading to biased estimates of quasi-normal mode parameters—particularly amplitude and phase—with reduced predictive accuracy compared to the arbitrary-polarization model.
\n\n
对于GW150914，受限模型仅使用两个LIGO探测器就成功测量了圆偏振度和手征性，从而仅凭铃宕信号即可推断倾角。相比之下，当应用于GW190521等进动系统时，同一模型无法捕捉真实的偏振结构，导致准正规模参数（尤其是振幅和相位）的估计出现偏差，且预测精度低于任意偏振模型。

Conclusion: Equatorial reflection symmetry provides a powerful simplification for modeling ringdown signals from non-precessing binary black hole mergers, enabling inclination measurements from polarization even with limited detector networks. However, this symmetry does not hold for precessing systems, and enforcing it introduces systematic errors in ringdown spectroscopy. As gravitational-wave detectors become more sensitive, adopting physically appropriate polarization models—especially for complex merger dynamics—is essential to avoid biased tests of general relativity and black hole spectroscopy.
\n\n
赤道反射对称性为建模非进动双黑洞并合的铃宕信号提供了有力简化，即使在有限探测器网络下也能通过偏振测量倾角。然而，该对称性在进动系统中不成立，强行施加会导致铃宕谱学中的系统误差。随着引力波探测器灵敏度提升，采用物理上恰当的偏振模型——尤其针对复杂并合动力学——对避免广义相对论检验和黑洞谱学中的偏差至关重要。

Abstract: Merging binary black holes exhibit a ringdown phase in which they primarily emit gravitational waves in the shape of damped sinusoids corresponding to quasi-normal modes of the Kerr remnant. In general, each mode carries four degrees of freedom encoding amplitude and phase information. When the modes are excited with equatorial reflection symmetry, as is the case for black hole mergers with spins (anti)aligned to the orbital angular momentum, the symmetry constrains two degrees of freedom. As a result, the relationship between polarization amplitudes and phases in each mode is fixed by the viewing (inclination) angle to the equatorial plane. We use such a constrained model to fit the ringdown signals of both non-precessing and precessing systems such as GW150914 and GW190521, respectively. We show that we can measure the degree of circular polarization and handedness of ringdown signals like those of GW150914, even if only the two LIGO detectors are available; such a polarization measurement can be translated into an inferred source inclination assuming the reflection symmetry above, again using the ringdown signal alone. On the other hand, the constrained polarization model is insufficient to capture the polarization structure of signals from precessing systems, leading to biases in the inferred mode frequencies and amplitudes. We explore the magnitude of this effect by fitting GW190521-like injections with the restricted model, finding weaker predictive accuracy relative to the arbitrary-polarization model and potentially significant systematic biases. As our detectors continue to improve, using the correct polarization model is increasingly important to avoid biased ringdown measurements.

### [3] [A metric solution for rotating black holes embedded in dark matter halos with central spikes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15330)
*Rui-Hong Yue,Yu-Qian Zhao,Wei-Liang Qian*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: The paper presents an exact analytic metric for rotating black holes embedded in generic dark matter halos featuring a central density spike truncated near the horizon. This spacetime is asymptotically flat, intrinsically anisotropic due to a metric discontinuity at the truncation radius, and generalizes the spherically symmetric Cardoso et al. solution to the rotating case.
\n\n
本文提出了一种精确的解析度规，用于描述嵌入具有中心密度尖峰的通用暗物质晕中的旋转黑洞。该时空是渐近平直的，由于在截断半径处存在度规不连续性而本质上是各向异性的，并将Cardoso等人提出的球对称解推广到了旋转情形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Modeling realistic astrophysical black holes requires incorporating surrounding dark matter distributions, especially those with density spikes induced by the black hole’s gravitational potential. Existing analytic metrics either neglect rotation or assume idealized, non-truncated dark matter profiles that do not respect physical constraints from adiabatic invariants. A physically motivated, rotating black hole metric coupled to a truncated dark matter spike is needed to study observable effects like lensing, accretion, and gravitational wave signatures.
\n\n
对现实天体物理黑洞进行建模需要考虑其周围的暗物质分布，特别是由黑洞引力势诱导产生的密度尖峰。现有的解析度规要么忽略自转，要么假设理想化的、未截断的暗物质轮廓，这不符合绝热不变量分析所给出的物理约束。因此，亟需一个物理上合理的、与截断型暗物质尖峰耦合的旋转黑洞度规，以研究透镜效应、吸积过程和引力波信号等可观测效应。

Method: The authors construct an exact solution to the Einstein field equations by introducing a rotating generalization of a dark matter energy-momentum tensor that features a central density spike truncated at a radius $r = r_t$ close to the event horizon. The truncation ensures that the energy density $\\rho(r)$ and both radial and tangential pressures vanish identically for $r > r_t$, consistent with adiabatic invariant arguments. The resulting metric is built to be asymptotically flat and reduces to known limits (e.g., Kerr, Cardoso et al.'s static halo) under appropriate parameter choices. Anisotropy arises from the discontinuity in the stress-energy tensor at $r = r_t$, which induces a corresponding discontinuity in the metric derivatives.
\n\n
作者通过引入一种具有中心密度尖峰的暗物质能量-动量张量的旋转推广形式，构建了爱因斯坦场方程的一个精确解。该尖峰在靠近事件视界的半径 $r = r_t$ 处被截断，从而确保在 $r > r_t$ 时能量密度 $\\rho(r)$ 以及径向和切向压强恒为零，这与绝热不变量的论证一致。所得度规被构造为渐近平直，并在适当参数选择下退化为已知情形（如Kerr度规、Cardoso等人提出的静态晕模型）。由于应力-能量张量在 $r = r_t$ 处不连续，导致度规导数也出现不连续，从而产生各向异性。

Result: The constructed metric successfully describes a rotating black hole surrounded by a physically motivated, truncated dark matter spike. It is asymptotically flat, exhibits intrinsic anisotropy due to the discontinuity at the truncation radius, and smoothly recovers the Kerr metric when the dark matter contribution is turned off. In the non-rotating limit, it reproduces the spherically symmetric metric of Cardoso et al. The model parameters are interpreted in terms of black hole mass, spin, and dark matter spike properties (central density, truncation radius).
\n\n
所构建的度规成功描述了一个被物理上合理的、截断型暗物质尖峰包围的旋转黑洞。该度规是渐近平直的，由于在截断半径处的不连续性而表现出内在各向异性，并且在关闭暗物质贡献时可平滑地恢复为Kerr度规。在无自转极限下，它重现了Cardoso等人提出的球对称度规。模型参数被解释为黑洞质量、自旋以及暗物质尖峰特性（中心密度、截断半径）。

Conclusion: This work provides the first exact, rotating black hole metric coupled self-consistently to a truncated dark matter halo with a central spike, offering a new theoretical framework for studying strong-field gravity in realistic galactic environments. The intrinsic anisotropy and metric discontinuity encode the physical effect of dark matter dynamics near the horizon, enabling more accurate modeling of phenomena such as black hole shadows, orbital precession, and gravitational wave emission in dark matter-rich regions.
\n\n
本工作首次提供了与具有中心尖峰的截断型暗物质晕自洽耦合的精确旋转黑洞度规，为在真实星系环境中研究强场引力提供了一个新的理论框架。其内在各向异性与度规不连续性编码了暗物质在视界附近动力学的物理效应，使得在富含暗物质的区域中对黑洞阴影、轨道进动和引力波辐射等现象的建模更加精确。

Abstract: We propose an analytic metric describing rotating black holes surrounded by generic dark matter halos. This metric is an exact solution of the field equations that incorporates a dark matter halo with a central density spike in the vicinity of the black hole. The dark matter profile is truncated at a radius close to the horizon, in accordance with analyses based on adiabatic invariants, so that the energy density as well as the radial and tangential pressures vanish identically beyond this point. The presence of the spike and the associated metric discontinuity implies that the spacetime is intrinsically anisotropic. The resulting geometry is asymptotically flat and reduces to several well-known cases under suitable limits. In particular, it generalizes the spherically symmetric metric proposed by Cardoso {\it et al.} to the case of rotating black holes. We discuss the physical interpretation of the model parameters and illustrate the metric by applying it to several specific gravitational systems.

### [4] [Detectability of avoided crossings in black hole ringdowns](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16199)
*Hayato Imafuku,Naritaka Oshita,Hiroki Takeda*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: Quasinormal modes (QNMs) of black holes can undergo avoided crossings (ACs), where closely spaced frequencies lead to enhanced amplitudes with opposite phases and interference. The paper shows that resolving individual QNMs in such scenarios is extremely challenging even with ideal data, but collective signatures of ACs may still be detectable if they dominate the ringdown and contamination from other modes is minimal.
\n\n
黑洞的准正规模（Quasinormal modes, QNMs）可能发生避免交叉（avoided crossings, ACs），此时频率接近的模式会表现出增强的振幅、近乎相反的相位并产生干涉。本文指出，即使在理想观测条件下，分辨单个QNM仍然极其困难；但如果AC相关的模式主导了铃宕信号（ringdown signal），且其他慢衰减模式的污染可忽略或可被移除，则AC的整体波形特征仍可能被探测到。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Avoided crossings (ACs) in black hole quasinormal mode spectra produce nearly degenerate complex frequencies with enhanced and out-of-phase amplitudes, generating distinctive interference patterns in gravitational waveforms. However, standard black hole spectroscopy assumes well-separated modes and struggles when modes become spectrally close. This work aims to assess whether current or near-future gravitational-wave observations can resolve or infer such nearly degenerate QNMs within a Bayesian inference framework.
\n\n
黑洞准正规模谱中的避免交叉（avoided crossings, ACs）会产生近简并的复频率，并伴随增强且反相的振幅，从而在引力波波形中形成独特的干涉图样。然而，标准黑洞谱学（black hole spectroscopy）假设各模式在频谱上是良好分离的，当模式变得非常接近时，该方法将面临挑战。本文旨在通过贝叶斯推断框架评估当前或近期引力波观测是否能够分辨或推断此类近简并QNM。

Method: The authors employ a Bayesian parameter estimation framework to analyze synthetic ringdown signals containing two nearly degenerate QNMs undergoing an avoided crossing. They use three distinct waveform models as templates: (i) a superposition of two damped sinusoids with independent complex amplitudes and frequencies, (ii) a physically motivated model incorporating AC-induced phase correlations, and (iii) an effective single-mode approximation. They systematically vary the frequency separation $\\Delta \\omega = |\\omega_1 - \\omega_2|$ and study how posterior distributions for complex frequencies and amplitudes depend on this separation and the choice of template.
\n\n
作者采用贝叶斯参数估计框架，分析包含两个经历避免交叉的近简并QNM的人造铃宕信号。他们使用三种不同的波形模型作为模板：(i) 两个具有独立复振幅和频率的阻尼正弦波的叠加；(ii) 一个引入AC诱导相位关联的物理驱动模型；(iii) 一种有效单模近似。他们系统地改变频率间隔 $\\Delta \\omega = |\\omega_1 - \\omega_2|$，并研究复频率与振幅的后验分布如何依赖于该间隔及模板选择。

Result: Resolving the two individual QNM frequencies is found to be highly challenging—even with high signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) and optimistic assumptions—due to strong parameter degeneracies and multimodal posteriors. However, when the AC-related modes dominate the ringdown and contamination from longer-lived (e.g., fundamental) modes is negligible or removed, certain collective features (e.g., amplitude enhancement and phase opposition) leave identifiable imprints in the waveform that can be captured by appropriately designed templates, particularly those encoding AC physics.
\n\n
研究发现，即使在高信噪比（SNR）和乐观假设下，由于强烈的参数简并与多峰后验分布，分辨两个独立QNM频率仍然极具挑战性。然而，当AC相关模式主导铃宕信号，且长寿命模式（如基模）的污染可忽略或已被移除时，某些集体特征（如振幅增强与相位相反）会在波形中留下可识别的印记，这些印记可通过适当设计的模板（尤其是编码了AC物理的模板）捕捉到。

Conclusion: While direct resolution of individual quasinormal modes involved in an avoided crossing is likely beyond the reach of current gravitational-wave detectors, indirect evidence for ACs may still be accessible through their collective waveform signatures. This requires both high-quality data and waveform models that explicitly account for the correlated amplitude-phase structure induced by avoided crossings. Future detectors with improved sensitivity could make such tests feasible.
\n\n
尽管直接分辨避免交叉中涉及的单个准正规模可能超出了当前引力波探测器的能力，但通过其整体波形特征仍可能获得AC存在的间接证据。这既需要高质量数据，也需要显式考虑避免交叉所诱导的振幅-相位关联结构的波形模型。未来更高灵敏度的探测器有望使此类检验成为可能。

Abstract: Quasinormal modes (QNMs) of black holes can exhibit avoided crossings (ACs), in which specific QNM frequencies approach each other while their amplitudes are enhanced and acquire nearly opposite phases, leading to characteristic interference. Resolving such closely spaced modes through black hole spectroscopy is observationally challenging. In this paper, we investigate the detectability of nearly degenerate QNMs in the presence of an AC within a Bayesian framework using three waveform models. We examine how the inference of the complex frequencies and amplitudes depends on the separation between the two QNM frequencies and on the choice of template waveform. We find that resolving the individual QNM frequencies is difficult even under optimistic conditions. On the other hand, collective waveform signatures associated with ACs may still be identified through complementary waveform descriptions, provided that the AC-related modes dominate the observed ringdown signal and contamination from more slowly damped modes is negligible or can be removed.
